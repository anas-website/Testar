How to Use This Tool

1 Design Your Shelves

Adjust the parameters to design your custom shelves. Set the number of shelves, dimensions, and spacing between each shelf. The 3D preview updates automatically as you change values.

2 Preview in 3D

Click "Generate 3D Shelves" to see your design in fullscreen 3D viewer. Use mouse/touch to rotate and zoom the view.

3 View in AR

Click "View in AR" to place your shelves in your physical space. The shelves will be fixed in position once placed.

AR Initialization

Please follow these steps to initialize AR mode:

  • Ensure your device supports AR
  • Allow camera access when prompted
  • Point camera at a flat surface
  • Tap to place your shelves
